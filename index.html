Minecraft Information Board

Minecraft is a popular video game that was created by Markus Persson and released by Mojang in 2011. Minecraft allows players to build and explore generated worlds made up of blocks. Players can mine for resources, craft items, build whatever they please, and fight various monsters/creatures or other players. Minecraft also has a variety of different game modes, including survival mode, creative mode, hardcore mode, and adventure mode. Minecraft is avaliable on a variety of platforms, including PC, game consoles (Xbox,Sony,Nintendo), and mobile devices.

Terraria Information Board

Terraria is a video game that was released in 2011 by the game studio Re-Logic. Terraria is a 2D sandbox game, that allows players to explore, build, and survive in a world made up of blocks. However, Terraria also has deeper elements of action and adventure as there are a variety of different enemies, boss fights, and world events that can occur. The game can be played both single-player and multiplayer with a range of difficulty options to choose from. Terraria is avaliable to play on a variety of platforms, including PC, game consoles (Xbox,Sony,Nintendo), and mobile devices.
